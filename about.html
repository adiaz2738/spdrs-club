About Page

Hi, my name is Anthony and I'm the creator of The MarketPal. The MarketPal is a website that focuses on creating a community for traders and investors to get along and talk about the stock market.

People have the ability to join the Discord server, called spdrsClub, and chat with other people that enjoying investing and trading. You can join the server by going to the navigation bar at the top of the page and clicking on the Discord icon on the far right hand side.

If you choose to, you can also donate to support the website and the server by either making a one time donation on the discord server or joining the Patreon. If you'd like to become a Patreon, just go to the front page of the website where you'll find the "Become a Patron" button. Just click on it and you can subscribe to the first tier which is $5 per month and you'll gain access to some additional features. I'm also open to suggestions, so if you do choose to donate, you can suggest what kind of content or features you'd like to see for Patrons and I'll try my best to make it happen if possible.
